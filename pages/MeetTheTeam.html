Welcome to WatLock

Meet The Team

WatLock is a multidisciplinary team consisting of over 50 students from faculties across the University of Waterloo. Our members are organized in three major teams: Mechanical, Electrical and Business/Communications.

 Executive Team

 Mechanical Team

The mechanical team is responsible for the design of all components and subsystems of the airlock. This includes all the essential systems within an airlock to support life on Mars such as depressurization, sealing, structural support systems as well as unique functionalities such as a modular structure allowing for easier transportation. Members on the team do a lot of drawings by hand and afterwards proceed to completing designs with simulations, modelling, and calculations using SolidWorks.

 Electrical Team

Violet Z, Jerry X, Jin L, Anish M, Judy C, Gurubaani M, Angel S, Tony L, Nam T, Aishwarya S, Rob C, Anya F, Rickson Y, Drasti M, Abhyuday B, Nikhil M

The electrical team plays a vital role in the physical design of the airlock. We focus on “mission critical” systems such as life support and pressure control. Our work focuses on the computerized system of our airlock and the autonomy of nearly all systems. Members of the team spend work mostly with microcontrollers, circuit design software and communication protocols. This work, along with that of the mechanical team allow us to build complex systems within the airlock that ensures safety of astronauts and their ability to focus on other tasks.

 Business + Communications Team

Nivedita S, Molly L, Shaahana N, Jason A, Tony H, Mellisa L

The business and communications team addresses the financial concerns of the team. Without adequate funding, building an airlock for a Mars colony would just be a dream. The team is responsible for maintaining the website, marketing the team throughout the University, and making pivotal project management decisions that to help the team as a whole.
