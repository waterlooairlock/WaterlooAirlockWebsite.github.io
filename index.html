University of Waterloo Airlock Design Team

The Challenge

WatLock is a student design team at the University of Waterloo that’s goal is to make an airlock for a mars colony. We will be competing in UBC’s Mars Colony Project Airlock Challenge in May 2019 for the design competition and then in May 2020 for the prototyping competition. To successfully compete in the competition, our team consists of students from a wide array of programs that will work together to solve the issues of space settlement.
